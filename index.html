FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers about rates, child seats, multi-stop trips, and everything else.

Airport transfers start at $79 flat rate for a luxury sedan to O'Hare and $69 to Midway. Premium SUV (Escalade) starts at $109. These are flat rates — no surge pricing, no hidden fees. The price you book is the price you pay, regardless of traffic or time of day.

Yes! We provide car seats and booster seats at no extra charge. Just let us know how many you need when booking, and we'll have them properly installed and ready in your vehicle before pickup.

Absolutely. Add as many stops as you need when booking — we'll build your route and give you an accurate flat rate upfront. Great for airport runs with multiple family members, bar crawls, wedding day logistics, or errands around the city.

We own our fleet. Every vehicle is company-owned, professionally maintained, and driven by our directly employed chauffeurs. We're not a dispatch service that subcontracts to random drivers. When you book YM, you get YM — our cars, our drivers, our standards.

We monitor your flight in real-time via FAA data. If your flight is early, delayed, or rerouted, we adjust your pickup time automatically. Domestic arrivals get 30 minutes free wait time; international arrivals get 60 minutes (for customs). Your chauffeur meets you at baggage claim with a name sign.

Cancellations made 24+ hours before pickup receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours are charged 50%. No-shows are charged in full. We understand plans change — call us and we'll do our best to accommodate.

No — gratuity is not included and is at your discretion. The standard is 15-20% for exceptional service. You can add it when booking or tip your chauffeur directly.
